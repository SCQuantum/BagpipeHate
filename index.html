About Us


The beauty of Scotland

Scotland is one of the best places on Earth. It has it all: beautiful vistas, amazing people, bagpipes, and of course lucky charms!

Scottish Lowlands

Scotland is defined by its many hills that keep it from being flat uninteresting, but also not to dramatic to be hard to navigate.

We care about Scotland

Every day, Scotland's future is being threatened by not a silent killer, but a very loud and obnoxious one. The bagpipe.

What do they do? Or what they don't do...

While they may seem harmless enough, bagpipes actually do threaten the future of our great nation by harming the ears of the unfortunate soul foolish enough to go within a 2 mile vicinity of the "instrument".


Leprecauns

One of the most talked about parts of Scotland are the mythical leprecauns. These creatures appear at night and steal gold and cereal, and are known to be quite vicious.

What can we do about it?

Usually, a scottish flamethrower will do the trick, but for the evasive ones, you may be required to find the pot of gold at the end of the rainbow and throw it all into the mouth of a volcano.
